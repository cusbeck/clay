A Little About Myself

As a child I was always drawing and developed a passion for comics, film and animation. In 1995, after watching a documentary of animation and special effects in film, I saw other artists using their imagination to create amazing characters and environments and that was their job. From that moment I wanted nothing more than to be an artist. After high school, I got an Associates Degree in Animation and Design from The Art Institute of Fort Lauderdale, FL.

After school I went into graphic design for a clothing company for a little bit then went to Whole Foods Market as a store graphic artist. Now I'm doing art for children's books, creating my own comics and Concept art. Still to this day I'll never forget that moment in '95 when I decided to let my imagination take over and led me to the creative lifestyle I have made for myself.
